background

I started my professional career training in librarianship. I shifted quickly to archivist, then to data analyst once I left graduate school and started working, to project manager, and now to fledgling programmer. All of my career paths have focused on facilitating access to information for others, whether directly in libraries and archives or indirectly through building tools or munging data.
